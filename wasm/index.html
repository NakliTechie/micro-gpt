"Source" indicates whether the number was measured locally on this machine (M4 Pro, 10P+4E) or quoted from the upstream repos as published. Important caveats: (1) cross-machine numbers (M5 Pro vs M4 Pro) are not apples-to-apples — M5 Pro single-stream is roughly 1.5–1.8× M4 Pro due to clock + IPC changes; (2) the C+NEON benchmark precomputes (token, pos) embedding + RMSNorm + Q/K/V lookup tables outside its timed loop, while the WASM forward computes that work every step. WASM-vs-C+NEON is therefore "browser WASM vs LUT-optimized native," not strict same-workload comparison.

What's running. The C source for the entire forward pass (~150 lines) is in microgpt_inf.c, compiled with emcc -O3 -msimd128 -ffast-math. Weights are 16,768 bytes of fp32 loaded into the WASM module at startup. The model architecture matches Karpathy's microGPT exactly: n_embd=16, n_head=4, block_size=16, vocab=27, one transformer block. Sampling uses temperature 0.5 multinomial.
